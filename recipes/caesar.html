The Last Caesar Salad Recipe You'll Ever Need

This is an unbelievable, restaurant-quality, creamy Caesar salad dressing that will make you swear off of anything store-bought again.

Great for your classic Caesar salad, or as a dip for all kinds of veggies. Will NOT disappoint! To serve, toss with chopped romaine in a salad bowl and shave Parmesan cheese on top of salad; season with salt, black pepper, and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.

Ingredients

  • 2 anchovy fillets
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup half and half
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

Steps

  1. Combine anchovies with garlic.
  2. Process mayo, parmesan, half-and-half, lemon juice.
  3. refrigerate for 1 hour.
  4. serve.
